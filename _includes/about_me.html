Trevx
Trevx is an Idea that I came of with my two friends to enhance the experience of the web audio content consumption, by providing a platform that index the "public" audio content and simplify its retrieval and provide a direct and easy user interface to consume/play the results items.
In a nutshell, what we did is that we built a good/clean audio "advisory engine", that index the public audio content provided by a crawler and provision, sort and categorize that audio files. Once the user search on any content the most relevant with the best quality will be shown to the user and play it. Also the user can see the rest of the search result and play them as well.
Technologies used: PHP(laravel), AZUR servers, Digital Ocean Servers, Javascript, Mysql, ElasticSearch.
